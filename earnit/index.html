EarnIt

An Android app that connects wants to have-tos.

closed testing
AndroidFree

We all have those things we really want but don't need, and a list of dreaded chores we keep avoiding. EarnIt connects the two. Pick a reward you've been craving, set a point cost, and link the tasks that should fund it. Crush the "ew" tasks, watch the balance grow, and claim your prize guilt-free. You earned it.

Use it alongside your regular to-do app. EarnIt is just for the stuff you actually want to treat yourself to. Add a home screen widget to stay motivated and track progress at a glance.

Your privacy matters: all your data stays right on your device. No account required, no internet connection needed, nothing sent anywhere.

What's in the app

  • Link tasks to a specific reward and earn points toward it as you go.
  • Set a point cost for each reward, manually or automatically.
  • Mark tasks as mandatory so the claim button only unlocks once the real work is done.
  • Add a home screen widget for at-a-glance progress and one-tap task logging.
  • Browse the Task Library for ready-made templates to get started fast.
  • Archive claimed rewards with a full log, then reactivate them to run again.
  • Choose from three colour themes, full dark mode, 9 mascots that unlock as you progress, and a daily quote to keep you going.
  • Export your data any time, import it back, or rely on Android's automatic backup.

EarnIt is currently in closed testing on Android. Interested in trying it?
